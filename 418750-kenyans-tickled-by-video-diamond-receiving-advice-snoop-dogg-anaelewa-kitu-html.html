Celebrated Tanzanian singer Diamond Platnumz is currently in the US working on his album.

Diamond has shown no signs of returning to his home country weeks after travelling to the US for the BET Awards.

During his trip, Diamond rubbed shoulders with notable musicians among the Akon, Busta Rhymes, Wiz Khalifa, Swizz Beats and most recently, Snoop Dogg.

In a video shared online, Diamond and Snoop were seen together in the studio as the Drop It Like It's Hot rapper gave him nuggets of wisdom.

In the conversation, Snoop advised Diamond not to watch the numbers his projects reach.

“I used to watch the numbers, then I blinked and fell off. You should not fall off, because there is somebody else who will come as you came.You gonna work hard because other people are after you. They want to be like you right now,” Snoop said.

The West Coast rapper further divulged that many people in his neighbourhood were drug dealers and gangsters, but he inspired them to change their lives.

“When I started rapping, they slowly put down their drugs and said they want to be managers, producers or be like me. So I changed the perspective. Now in my hood, people are living well,” he said.

Throughout Snoop's dialogue, Diamond nodded his head and occasionally said yes. Netizens reacting to the video wondered whether the Wasafi Classic Baby (WCB) co-founder understood what Snoop was saying.
