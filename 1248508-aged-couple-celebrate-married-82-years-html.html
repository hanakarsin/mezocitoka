Reactions as aged couple celebrate being married for 82 years

 Posted on July 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  347 words  |  Lourie Helzer

Love is many things but in marriage, it is the fuel that keeps two partners running smoothly. When a couple stay together through thick and thin and manage to remain united for many years, their relationship is celebrated because it shows the beauty of being in love with someone else.

Legit.ng earlier shared the story of a certain couple identified as D.W. Williams and Willie William who had the internet believing in the powerful force of love and the strong will of commitment as they marked their 82nd wedding anniversary with love, peace and happiness.

The man, aged 103 and the lady aged 100, have been together for over eight decades and have nothing but a rare friendship and even more love as they celebrate their life together. It is not everyday one wakes up to the news of a couple proving that true love is a huge thing and two people can indeed spend the rest of their lives together.
