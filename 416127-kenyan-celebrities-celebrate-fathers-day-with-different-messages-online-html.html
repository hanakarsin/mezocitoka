Kenyan Celebrities Celebrate Father's Day With Heartwarming Messages Online

 Posted on July 9, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  614 words  |  Lourie Helzer
  • As Kenyans took time to honour Father's Day of 2021 through lovely messages on social media, celebrities too joined the fray
  • Most celebrated their spouses for being present fathers who have played active roles in raising their children
  • Others simply recognized the men who have sired children with them but are not active participants in their upbringing

Father's Day 2021 fell on Sunday, June 20, a day that saw individuals around the world celebrate fathers for their role in society and childbearing.

Social media handles of Kenyan celebrities were awash with celebratory messages to fathers, some honouring their biological dads while others penned messages to the men who fathered the children they are raising.

TUKO.co.ke took a run through some of these messages and compiled the list below:

Wahu

Secular-turned-gospel artist Wahu Kagwi showered praises on fellow singer and husband Nameless for being present in raising the couple's daughters.

"Thank you for making them feel like the young queens that they are. You are an amazing man and I'm so glad I get to do life and raise the girls with you," she wrote.

She asked her fans to join her in wishing the Megarider singer a wonderful Father's Day.

Akothee

Musician Akothee shared an Instagram post dedicated to the father of her three daughters.

She added that many times she thinks of letting go of his memories, but then it dawns on her that it is him who fathered three of her amazing children.

"Thank you for the three intelligent gifts you gave to me. You still remain the biological father to my three daughters. May God be with you and your family daddy, we love and cherish you Jared baba," read part of her post.

Julie Gichuru

For media personality Julie Gichuru, the month of June usually comes with many blessings as, apart from Father's Day, the household celebrates the birthday of the man of the house as well as their eldest son.

"So thankful for you both. So thankful and so blessed," she captioned the photo of father and son cutting a birthday cake.

Zinzi Khalwale

The daughter of politician Boni Khalwale didn't have many words for the former Senator Kakamega County.

She simply uploaded a photo of him and captioned it as the Greatest of All Time (G.O.A.T.)

Zinzi, who is a spitting image of her father is the mother of the politician's granddaughter.

Fridah Ongili

The wife of Embakasi East MP Babu Owino was full of praise for her husband and father of their children.

She shared a recent occurrence where the politician spent hours trying to create a toy car for their daughter.
