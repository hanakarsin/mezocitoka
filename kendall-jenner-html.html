Kendall Jenner - Age, Family, Bio

 Posted on February 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  225 words  |  Hettie Henneman

Model who has walked the runway at the world's top fashion weeks for such prominent designers as Chanel, Marc Jacobs, Diane Von Furstenberg, and many others. She first gained fame through her appearances on the E! reality TV show Keeping Up with the Kardashians, debuting on the series at age 11. After Keeping Up with the Kardashians ended in 2021, she joined her family on Hulu's The Kardashians.

Before Fame

She first began modeling at the age of 14, signing with Wilhelmina Models. 

Trivia

She became a brand ambassador for Seventeen Magazine. She and her sister Kylie launched a clothing line called Kendall & Kylie. 

Family Life

She was born to parents Kris Jenner and Caitlyn Jenner and raised alongside her sister Kylie. She has three half-sisters, Khloe, Kourtney and Kim Kardashian. Her half-brothers are Brandon Jenner, Brody Jenner and Rob Kardashian. In 2020, she began a relationship with Devin Booker. The couple later separated in June 2022 but got back together in July 2022. Booker and Jenner announced their separation again in November 2022. She dated Harry Styles from 2013 until 2014. She also dated NBA stars Blake Griffin and Ben Simmons and model Anwar Hadid. In early 2023, she started dating Bad Bunny until the end of the same year.

Associated With

She has been good friends with Hailey Bieber, along with models such as Cara Delevingne, Karlie Kloss, and Gigi Hadid.
