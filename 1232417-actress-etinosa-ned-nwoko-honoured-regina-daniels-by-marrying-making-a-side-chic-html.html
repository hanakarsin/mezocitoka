Actress Etinosa says Ned Nwoko honoured Regina Daniels by marrying her instead of making her a side

 Posted on May 17, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  315 words  |  Hettie Henneman

- Nollywood actress, Etinosa Idemudia, has reacted to the news of Reginal Daniel's marriage to 60-year-old billionaire, Ned Nwoko

- According to the actress, she sees nothing wrong as Nwoko honoured the teen actress rather than keep her a side chic

- She revealed this in a video which she shared on her Instagram stories

Earlier on, Legit.ng reported the news of how reports surfaced online which claimed that Nollywood teen actress, Regina Daniel secretly got married to a 60-year-old billionaire/politician, Ned Nwoko.

Following these reports and Ned Nwoko's recent statements, many Nigerians have taken to social media to react to the news, criticizing and condemning the actress. However, actress Etinosa Idemudia seems to feel differently.

The actress, in a recent video shared on her Instagram story, revealed that she sees nothing wrong in the teen actress getting married to the much older man as a sixth wife. According to her, he honoured Daniels by getting married to her rather than keep her as a side chic.
