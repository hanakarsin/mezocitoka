Atikus Aide, Bwala Meets Tinubu In France (Photo)

 Posted on February 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  301 words  |  Hettie Henneman

A presidential campaign spokesperson for Atiku Abubakar during the 2023 elections, Daniel Bwala, on Monday, met with President Bola Tinubu in Paris, France.

In a post via his official X handle, Bwala described President Tinubu as the father of the nation, saying that it was a pleasure to meet with the Nigerian leader in Paris.

According to him, President Tinubu continues to share his thoughts and demonstrate his passion for solving the economic and security challenges facing the nation.

He wrote: “It was a pleasure to meet with the father of the nation @officialABAT President Bola Tinubu in Paris today. He continues to share his thoughts and demonstrate his passion to lead Nigeria out of the woods.”

See the photo below.

Recall that Bwala had met with President Tinubu on January 10 at the Aso Rock Villa in Abuja and declared his availability to work with the administration of President Tinubu.

According to him, if supporting Tinubu means joining the All Progressives Congress (APC), he is ready to join the party and dump the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP).

Speaking to State House Correspondents after meeting Tinubu, Bwala said: “I told him today, I am committing to play my part to support your administration, and I have no apologies to anybody.

“APC is a party. President Bola Tinubu is my motivation; if supporting him will take me to APC, so be it,” he stated when asked by journalists if he would defect to the APC.

Speaking on the visit, Bwala took to his account on the X platform to express delight at meeting the President.

He wrote: “Today I was delighted to meet @officialABAT, President Bola Ahmed Tinubu, (GCFR) in the Villa to show appreciation and support for the bold decisions he is taking to deal with the crisis confronting our dear country.”
