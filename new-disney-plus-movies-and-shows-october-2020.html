New Disney Plus Movies and Shows Coming October 2020: A Complete List

 Posted on July 17, 2024  |  9 minutes  |  1716 words  |  Hettie Henneman

Here's a List of Everything That's Coming to Disney Plus in October 2020

By Ricky Pinela

Updated March 22 2021, 12:39 p.m. ET

With October just around the corner, many people are wondering what new titles will be available on the streaming platform Disney Plus. Here's what's coming soon to your screens at home.

Oct. 1

Here are the titles that will be available on Oct. 1, 2020.

'Maleficent'

Maleficent is the story of one of Disney’s biggest villains, portrayed by award-winning actress Angelina Jolie. The movie also stars Elle Fanning as Aurora, also known as Sleeping Beauty.

'Beverly Hills Chihuahua'

This 2008 movie centers around a wealthy Beverly Hills chihuahua named Chloe who gets lost and enlists in help to find her way home. The talking-dog movie stars Drew Barrymore, George Lopez, Piper Perabo, Jamie Lee Curtis, and many more.

'Cheaper by the Dozen 2'

The 2005 sequel to Steve Martin’s Cheaper by the Dozen, ushers in the return of a fan-favorite cast featuring Bonnie Hunt, Tom Welling, Hilary Duff, Alyson Stoner, with the addition of relative newcomer (at the time) Taylor Lautner and Schitt’s Creek’s Eugene Levy. In the movie, the Baker family competes with a rival family while on vacation.

'Mr. Holland's Opus'

According to IMDb, Mr. Holland’s Opus is about a frustrated composer who finds fulfillment as a high school music teacher. Starring Richard Dreyfuss and Glenne Headly as Glenn and Iris Holland, the movie has received raving reviews since its release in 1995.

'Secrets of the Zoo: Down Under' (Season 1)

The first season of the show shows a behind-the-scenes look at what goes on at Sydney, Australia’s largest zoo, the Taronga Zoo at Sydney Harbour.

'The Simpsons' (Season 31)

The 31st season of the cult-classic cartoon series features 22 episodes that aired between Sept. 29, 2019 and May 17, 2020.

Zenimation (Extended Edition Premiere)

Walt Disney Animation Studios Zenimation is an animated experience with the intention of letting viewers relax and unplug, This new release will feature long-form versions of the original pieces. 

'Magic of Disney's Animal Kingdom' (Episode 102)

Magic of Disney’s Animal Kingdom is a documentary series by Disney and National Geographic which takes viewers behind the scenes with animal-care experts, veterinarians and biologists at Disney’s Animal Kingdom park in Orlando, Fla. 

'One Day at Disney' (Episode 144)

A 52-part shorts series that each focus on a different Walt Disney employee's job. (via IMDb)

'Weird But True' (Episode 308)

Join hosts Charlie and Kirby Engelman as these siblings and science-lovers explore the fun and curious ways our world works. (via IMDb)
