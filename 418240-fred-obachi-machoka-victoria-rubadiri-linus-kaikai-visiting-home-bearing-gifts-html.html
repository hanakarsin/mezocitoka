Fred Obachi Machoka Thanks Victoria Rubadiri, Linus Kaikai for Visiting His Home Bearing Gifts

 Posted on April 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  357 words  |  Enda Jantzen
  • Veteran radio and TV presenter Fred Obachi Machoka is elated after his two colleagues paid him a visit at his home
  • Media personalities Victoria Rubadiri and Linus Kaikai visited the veteran journalist and carried him gifts
  • Machoka and his wife expressed immense joy on their visit and thanked the two celebrities for their visit

Veteran radio and TV presenter Uncle Fred Obachi Machokaa has thanked his two colleagues, Victoria Rubadiri and Linus Kaikai for their surprise visit to his rural home bearing gifts.

The two top TV personalities did not just visit with their bare hands but they carried gifts to please their colleague Machoka.

Machoka was honoured by their visit and after treating them to a deluge of food he took to social media to thank them.

In a Facebook post sighted by TUKO.co.ke, Machoka and his wife Sophie were highly elated by the visit.

"A pleasant surprise visit from my friends and colleagues truly lifted my spirits and put a smile on my beloved wife Mama Santos' face," said Machoka."They remembered that it is a good African tradition to carry a gift with you when visiting an elder! God bless you abundantly dear friends, you made our week," he added.

Fred Obachi Machoka and wife celebrate 40th wedding anniversary

Not too long ago, the Roga Roga host and his lovely wife Sophie decided to add a twist to their 40th wedding anniversary.

And instead of the common flowers and chocolates to commemorate the auspicious occasion, the two threw an elaborate wedding ceremony so they could celebrate their thriving love.

As was seen in Machakos Governor Alfred Mutua’s Facebook post, It was an invite-only affair held at Fred’s Isinya ranch dubbed Roga Roga ranch.

Everyone in the ceremony was full of glee as they watched a couple that has been immersed in love for 40 blissful years make their promise to stick together yet again.
