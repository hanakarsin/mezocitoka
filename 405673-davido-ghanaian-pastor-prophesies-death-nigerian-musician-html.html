- A Ghanaian pastor, Prince Elisha Osei Kofi, has claimed that he saw a vision in which Nigerian singer, Davido, was shot dead

- He said this happened during a night event at a pub or a club where the singer had visited with his friends

- Osei said he saw top Nigeria public figures being killed as well

- He called on all to wake up in prayers for Davido and the whole of Nigeria

- Nigerian fans have commented on the development and have not taken it lightly

Ghanaian pastor, Prince Elisha Osei Kofi, has claimed that he saw popular Nigerian superstar, Davido, being shot dead.

He said, in a vision, he saw that Davido was shot at an evening event resembling a nightclub, or a pub, where he had gone with his entourage to have fun.

Osei called on Ghanaians and Nigerians to wake up in prayers on behalf of the Gobe hitmater to avert the impending calamity on his life.

However, he did not see only Davido in the vision. Osei said he saw top Nigerian figures also dead in Nigeria.

"I saw in the vision Davido being shot. They were going to shoot Davido at a night club or an event that took place at night. They rushed him to the hospital but he died. That young man is a target of the enemies who want him dead. I also saw Nigeria in chaos," he claimed.

According to him, there was vision, mayhem, and confusion in the whole country as the massacre was ongoing.

He made a prayer in the video saying that Davido and his countrymen will not die and that he cancels all evil plans against him and those he saw in the video.
