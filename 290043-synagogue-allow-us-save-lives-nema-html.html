Synagogue Did Not Allow To Us Save Lives NEMA

 Posted on February 7, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  246 words  |  Hettie Henneman

The National Emergency management Agency, NEMA, has revealed that the Synagogue of Nations Church stopped them from doing their job.

This was revealed by the spokesperson of the agency, Ibrahim Familoye, who talked to PREMIUM TIMES last night, September 12.

The two storey building was being remodeled with extra three floors being added.
