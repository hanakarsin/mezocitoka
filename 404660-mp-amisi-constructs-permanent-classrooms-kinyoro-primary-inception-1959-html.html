Saboti MP Caleb Amisi has constructed the first modern tuition block at Kinyoro Primary School since it was established in 1959.

Amisi has been actively revamping the education sector in Saboti constituency by constructing new classrooms, providing modern facilities, awarding bursaries, and purchasing school buses, among other investments.

The lawmaker noted Kinyoro Primary was one of the oldest schools in Saboti and had played a vital role in shaping the lives of many people from the constituency.

The school, according to Amisi, the school has also played a pivotal role in fostering peaceful co-existence between Bukusus and Saboats who neighbour each other and quench their thirsty for education at the institution.

Despite this, the lawmaker noted the previous leadership has disregarded its value and left it at the mercy of nature.

The past leadership left Saboti behind in terms of development for so long, they ignored to invest in education completely. Every school I go to, they say I am the first sitting mp to visit since inception," said Amisi.

Past leaders include Wafula Wabuke, the late Wamalwa Kijana (who also served as Kenya's eighth vice president), George Nakitare, Eugene Wamalwa (current Devolution CS) and David Wafula.

The school had been neglected since independence but it's a new dawn for the community around as I have channelled funds through the NG-CDF Saboti Constituency to totally change the face of that institution which is serving majority of children in that Ward," said the MP.

As a result of his dedication to improving the education sector, uniting locals, and empowering the youth, Amisi was crowned by elders as the Saboat community leader.

"I'm proud to be a pillar uniting all the different tribes in my constituency despite our political affiliations. In Saboti Constituency we are united towards one main course which is development" he acknowledged.

Amisi made history as the first MP to defy the FORD Kenya wave and won a parliamentary seat on the ODM party ticket, making him the only MP elected on the Raila Odinga-led outfit in Trans Nzioa.
