'Sexy Old Man' - Helen Paul Praises RMD As He Turns Up In An Exquisite Outfit At The AMVCA Awards

 Posted on March 20, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  210 words  |  Enda Jantzen

Popular Nigerian comedienne, Helen Paul has praised Nollywood veteran actor Richard Mofe-Damijo for the way he treats others.

The comedienne took to her Instagram account to post a video of herself and the veteran actor at the 2022 AMVCA Awards.

In the video, the comedian and the veteran actor who as usual look well put in an exquisite outfit, exchanged pleasantries happily.

Doing a voice over in the video, Helen praised the actor for treating others well irrespective of their status.

She explained that Mofe-Damijo often times bring people to his level notwithstanding his outstanding status as a veteran and went ahead to call him a sexy old man.
