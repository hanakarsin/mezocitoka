Who Has Michael B. Jordan Dated? A Look at the Actor's Exes

 Posted on May 30, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  616 words  |  Enda Jantzen

See a list of Michael B. Jordan’s exes — real or rumored — amid the news that he and Lori Harvey, Steve Harvey’s daughter, have split up.

Ladies and gentlemen all over the world are marveling at the latest relationship news surrounding People magazine's 2020 Sexiest Man Alive, aka Michael B. Jordan. It appears that Michael and longtime girlfriend, Lori Harvey, have officially called their relationship quits.

Michael and Lori, who went official with their relationship in January 2021, were one of Hollywood's "it" couples. However, things have gone left, and social media users are chiming in.

Now that Lori is officially added to the Creed star's list of exes, it's only right that we take a deep dive into the actor's romantic past. Here's the rundown on Michael B. Jordan's exes — both confirmed and rumored — along with some tea on his breakup with Lori.

Lori Harvey

Michael and Lori first set off coupledom rumors around Thanksgiving 2020 after being spotted at an Atlanta airport. The couple were then spotted at a Salt Lake City airport before New Year's 2021. They later went on to confirm their relationship on Jan. 10, 2021, with respective Instagram posts.

However, the pair called it quits in June 2022. Sources told People that Michael was ready to take their relationship to the next level, but it seems that Lori was not quite there yet.

"He let down his guard with her, opening up emotionally in a romantic relationship for the first time," a source told the publication. "They had great times together and brought out the best in each other."

The source continued, "Michael and Lori are both completely heartbroken. They still love each other."

Cindy Bruna

This model — who has posed for Victoria’s Secret, Prada, and Calvin Klein — was photographed leaving New York City restaurant Carbone with Michael in May 2019. And he was all smiles in the paparazzi pics!

KiKi Layne

Michael and KiKi — an actress famous for her roles in If Beale Street Could Talk and The Old Guard — reportedly got close at a Sundance party at the Tao nightclub pop-up in Park City, Utah, in January 2019. “Michael and KiKi were super flirty all night,” an eyewitness told People at the time. “He spent literally all night talking and focused on her.” (Meanwhile, a source told Us Weekly that the actor was “kissing [KiKi] on the cheek” before they “left together in the same car.”)

Kendall Jenner

Photographs of Michael leaving the Met Gala with this model and reality star in May 2015 sparked relationship rumors — and backlash, as the actor told GQ later that year. “A lot of Black fans were feeling like, ‘Oh, my God, he should have been with a Black woman’ and that whole thing,” he explained. “I get it, but on the other hand it’s, like, relax. You know — it’s 2015. It’s OK! People can like one another, not necessarily from the same history or culture or whatever the f--k it is. It’s just the new world, you know what I mean?”

Catherine Paiz

The Black Panther star was spotted enjoying a day in the sun with a “mystery girl” — later identified as Catherine Paiz, a social media influencer — on a Miami beach in January 2014.
