Zari Hassan Officially Declares She's Single Again, Not Searching

 Posted on August 6, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  352 words  |  Enda Jantzen
  • South Africa based Ugandan socialite Zari Hassan publicly stated that she is single again
  • This came after one of her fans asked her whether she is single or taken; she replied to the fan that she is single and not searching
  • Not so long ago, Zari gave her fans a hint that she was seeing Dark Stallion

South Africa based Ugandan socialite Zari Hassan popularly known as the Boss lady, has declared that she is single yet again and not searching.

On Sunday, August 15, Zari posted cute photos of herself on Instagram enjoying cool moments at the port.

The post attracted different comments from her fans, but one comment from a fan who wanted to know whether Diamond's baby mama is in a relationship or not stood out.

The fan, identified as @iamofficialanwar apologetically asked Hassan:

"Sorry to ask but for now, are you single or taken?"

The Boss lady replied that she is single and not searching.

This is how Hassan captioned the photos:

"Work smart not hard, live your best, worry less and believe you still deserve better in everything."

Zari previously had a relationship with Tanzanian crooner Diamond Platinumz and they had two children together, namely, Latifah Dangote and Prince Nillan.

Rumours recently did rounds that Zari is in a new relationship even though she didn't flaunt it like her previous relationship with Diamond keeping, but if her reply to the comment of her fan is anything to go by, then she is single again and not searching.

Zari, Lover Dark Stallion Reunite

As previously reported by TUKO.co.ke, Zari shared a message on her Insta stories, hinting that she had parted ways with her lover Dark Stallion.

However, 2 weeks later, Zari and Stallion visited her manager Galston Anthony's family. The manager shared a video he took with his wife and their baby together with Zari and her man.

Zari and her man looked happy together and a few moments before they left Anthony's house, they hugged each other tightly.
