Through the hashtag, #HighSchoolConfessions, which has since topped the Kenyan Twitter trends for a second day running, the tweeps reminisced about their secondary school life.

According to the tweets sampled by TUKO.co.ke, the netizens unanimously agreed that their Mathematics teachers hardly missed their lessons and would show up when least expected.

Some social media users recalled how their Mathematics and Chemistry teachers would hijack their Physical Education (PE) lessons to teach 'something serious'.

"I know it's lunchtime but let me introduce something small about..." was a common phrase for the teachers who would eat into the lunch break time.

Reactions

Here are some of the hilarious comments sampled by TUKO.co.ke:

With limited tea break time and long queues witnessed at the canteen, acquiring bread or Mandazi required some muscles and strategies or else you would end up 'whistling' even with your loaded pockets.

Another user Tweeted about the notorious backbenchers who hardly followed the rules. They repeatedly made it to the noisemakers list.

Another tweep recalled how students would copy their fellow learners' work. That was not a problem. It turned worse when the teacher could ask you to explain how you arrived at your answer.
