Does bitter kola have caffeine?

Actually, there are more benefits of eating bitter kola than its disadvantages. Doctors say that people who eat this plant can treat cold, lung diseases, malaria, food poisoning, improve their immunity and so on. The medical workers and scientists also say that bite kola contains a little of caffeine. Generally, it shouldn’t be harmful for human’s body because its level is really quite low. However, there are the cases when even a little caffeine level can lead to unpleasant consequences.

Negative impact of caffeine in bitter kola

Firstly, it has a negative impact on nervous system. Most disputes arise about the effect of caffeine on the heart and blood vessels. It was found that caffeine in bitter kola can cause short-term increase in pulse and blood pressure. That’s why people suffering from cardiovascular diseases can’t eat bitter kola nuts in large quantities.

Long-term studies have shown that caffeine can cause a slight increase in pressure, but it does not affect the development of hypertension. Moreover, caffeine content of bitter kola is useful for those who eat it in small quantities. In this case it helps to train their cardiovascular system.
