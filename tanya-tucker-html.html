Tanya Tucker - Age, Family, Bio

 Posted on March 29, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  118 words  |  Hettie Henneman

Country singer whose first #1 hit was "Delta Dawn," which she released at the age of thirteen. She has been nominated for multiple Country Music Association awards.

Before Fame

She was given a chance to sing at the Arizona State Fair after impressing the fair's entertainment managers. Her family moved to Las Vegas in 1969 and she performed there.

Trivia

She founded her own record label, Tuckertime Records, in 2002.

Family Life

She had a daughter, Presley Tanita and son, Beau "Grayson" with actor Ben Reed. She later had another daughter named Layla LaCosta with on-off partner and musician Jerry Laseter.

Associated With

She recorded several songs with Glen Campbell, whom she also briefly dated from 1980-1981.
