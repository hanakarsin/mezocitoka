Lady In Coma After Encounter With Suspected Ritualists In Osun

 Posted on February 28, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  442 words  |  Almeda Bohannan

A lady confirmed to be a prostitute is reportedly in a coma after having a heated argument with some men suspected to be ritualists in Osun State.

Naija News learnt that the resident of Ofatedo met with the suspects who swindled her away from a hotel around West bypass, Osogbo to another location to molest her and possibly attempted to use her for rituals.

Some residents of Adetunji Estate who reportedly communicated The Punch Metro explained that the lady whose identity is yet to be confirmed was dumped by some unknown men at the entrance to the street late on Friday.

Giving further details on the incident, one of the residents who gave her name as Tope, explained that they saw the lady battling to move her body after she was dropped by a motorcycle in the area.

“Immediately, men of the Operation Amotekun were contacted,” Tope noted during the interview.

Confirming the incident in a statement, the Field Commander, Osun Amotekun Corps, Mr. Amitolu Shittu, said he led the team that visited the scene after receiving a distress call from the residents.

Shittu explained that the victim started showing strange symptoms by vomiting blood while trying to narrate the incident to the command after regaining consciousness.

According to him, the victim was able to explain to the corps command what transpired between her and the three suspected ritualists, who had taken her from another hotel located in the Ofatedo area.

Shittu explained further saying; “I led the team that visited entrance to Adetunji Estate that evening. Before she started vomiting blood, the lady told me she was living in Ofatedo, though an indigene of Sagamu in Ogun State.

“She said three men had taken her from another hotel in Ofatedo and took her to a hotel around West Bye pass. At the hotel, they told her she would have sex with the three of them, but the lady said she told them she could only have sex with two men.

“While bathing with one of the men, the suspected ritualist hit the lady with a charm on the head and she became almost unconscious.”

“She was still talking to me when she started vomiting blood. Because it was late, I asked the night guard working in the area to watch over her, while I arrange an ambulance. By the time I returned to the scene, that her friend she told me of, had brought a bus to carry her. It was such a pathetic story.”

Shittu said operatives of the corps already had some leads through the mobile phone of the victim and promised that the suspects would be apprehended.
