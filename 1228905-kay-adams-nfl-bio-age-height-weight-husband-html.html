Career

In high school, Kay developed an interest in the media. After graduating from college, she worked in a few places before joining the NFL Network. One of her first jobs was as an in-game host for the St. Louis Cardinals.

Later, she joined fantasy football shows, including DirecTV's NFL Sunday Ticket Fantasy Zone, SiriusXM's Livin' the Fantasy, and SiriusXM Fantasy Drive. She also worked at FanDuel and NBC Sports Network.

In 2016, she joined the NFL Network. She hosted Good Morning Football on Mondays through Fridays. She also covered the Chicago Bears during special events. On 17th September 2018, it was announced that she would be joining the DAZN boxing broadcast team.
