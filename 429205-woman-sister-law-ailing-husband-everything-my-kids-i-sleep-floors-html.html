My kids and I have undergone cruelty from my in-laws; I never imagined I could.

I am a mom to two kids. My firstborn child belongs to a man who died years back when my son was just three months old. Then I met my current husband whom we have one kid together.

It all started last November on a Saturday when my husband did not come home as usual.

At first, I thought he was delaying somewhere, but I got worried when he did not call to tell me where he was. His phone was off too.

A two-week search for missing husband

I informed his family about his missing on the morning of the following day. We desperately searched for him for two weeks. I couldn’t sleep well; I kept wondering what might have happened to him.

The kids were restless too; they missed their daddy.

Hubby found in a coma in hospital

We searched everywhere, even mortuaries. Luckily we found him at the Kenyatta National Hospital (KNH). But he was in a coma. He couldn’t remember anything.

This is where my painful journey began.

I did not have money, but I had to see him at the hospital daily. I had to walk every day from Kangemi to Kawangware because I did not have the money to pay for the whole trip. So I thought shortening the distance would allow me to pay a lesser amount.

I always found him in a bad state. He would have soiled himself, and I would clean that up. I would also use a syringe to feed him milk.

Soon he regained consciousness and was ready to be discharged. The bill was KSh 114,000, and I spoke to the social workers at the hospital to help me with the bill, and it worked.

I was so grateful that finally, I would go home with my husband. The kids had missed him a lot. They kept asking me where daddy was, but I always assured them he would soon be home.

On reaching home, I knew I had to stop running my small business to take care of him as he was not completely healed.

So I stayed home with him taking care of him. He had lost his memory because he had sustained head injuries, yet he does not remember what happened to him. He doesn’t even remember how he got the injuries.

One day, his elder sister came to see him at home but ended up lashing out at me, saying I was not taking care of their brother well enough.

She insulted me in front of him and my kids, and he did not defend me. I was heartbroken though I kept holding on, hoping he would be alright soon and we could continue raising our children together.

Sister-in-law from hell

But the sister kept coming and saying nasty things to me. At one point, she said that I wanted to kill their brother the same way I killed my previous husband. This was very hurtful, to say the least.

I had not shared my story with her about the death of my first husband, so I do not know how she found out.

In May this year, she came to the house with a truck and took her brother and everything in the house.

She did not leave even a spoon and took their brother away from me, the father of my children. I had to sleep on the floor with my kids for three days.

Thats when my sister came to my house and helped me move in with her. But I couldn’t live with her for too long because her house is small and she lives with her husband and kids.

I also wanted to start my business and be back on my feet again for the sake of my children.

If you know someone who can offer any job opportunities, house girl, cleaning or any other, please help me for the sake of my kids.

Pleading for help

Also, anyone willing to gift me a bed and a mattress, even if they are old, I will appreciate it so much. My boy is asthmatic, and the cold is really affecting him.

Every day I get more desperate. I just feel like going to the streets and earning a living from doing anything they do there. I have tried but not getting any stable job, and the landlord is on my neck. It’s so frustrating.

My husband contacts me occasionally, but because his sister is housing him and giving him the money, he hides it from her. She would be furious to find out that we are still communicating, so he follows what she says.
