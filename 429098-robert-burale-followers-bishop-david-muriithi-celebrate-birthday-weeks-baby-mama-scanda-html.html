Followers of Bishop David Muriithi Celebrate His Birthday Weeks after Baby Mama Scandal

 Posted on May 11, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  541 words  |  Lourie Helzer
  • Bishop Muriithi has been keeping a low profile ever since he was exposed as a deadbeat father
  • The father of three has been the subject of discussion online after his baby mama Judy Mutave sued him for failing to pay child support for their two-year-old son
  • Celebrating a new year, his followers took to social media to shower him with love on his special day and Pastor Burale was among them

Senior pastor of House of Grace Church Bishop David Murithii has turned a year older.

The father of three has been the subject of discussion online after his baby mama Judy Mutave sued him for failing to pay child support for their two-year-old son.

In court papers seen by TUKO.co.ke, the mother of one said she wanted KSh 100,000 per month for child support, which would cater for several utilities, including food, rent, clothing, entertainment, and toys. The man of God responded through his lawyer, saying he could only afford KSh 10,000 per month.
