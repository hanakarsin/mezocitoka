Humour is needed now and then to help people unwind and catch some break from life. Watching funny movies is one way of relaxing and consuming humour. African American comedy movies are known to be funny with a certain degree of hilarity that is only distinct to the black community. Many directors, movie producers and actors who work on black movies fuse in hilarious lines and funny sketches that the black community can relate to.

Comedy movies with black actors are a must-watch for many as the actors give their all. Whether it is a Spike Lee movie or a Tyler Perry movie, you know a movie that adopts a theme associated with the black community will crack you up before you watch. Many actors who get featured to become popular in their communities and are seen as role models for many aspiring black actors.
